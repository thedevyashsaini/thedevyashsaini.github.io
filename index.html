About me

Hey, I'm Devyash—a tech tinkerer, design geek, and occasional organizer of fun (read: slightly chaotic) events. I turn caffeine into code, chaos into cool projects, and occasional witty one-liners. Stick around; it gets nerdier! I dabble in everything from crafting sleek UI/UX designs and building sturdy backends (supported by DevOPs pipelines) to diving into the mind-bending world of Generative AI.

My goal? To build stuff that not only works like a charm but also makes you think, 'Huh, that's pretty neat.' Whether it's a quirky side project or a big event, I'm all about blending creativity, logic, and a healthy dose of humor.

Things I haven't explored yet (but hey, who's counting): Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Quantum Computing, AR/VR… and probably time travel (still working on the flux capacitor)."
